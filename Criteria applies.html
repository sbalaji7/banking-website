What makes up our total interest rate

Your combined savings balance (of all your Save accounts) will fall into

different tiers with a corresponding bonus interest rate. The tiers mean

that different amounts of your savings will earn different rates

depending on which tier they’re in if you meet the bonus criteria.

monthly. No interest is payable if the bonus criteria are not met. See what makes you eligible for bonus interest.

Want bonus interest on all your Save accounts?

All you need to do is have a Spend account and deposit $500+ per month into
any Spend, Bills, or Save accounts (not including internal transfers) to get the
bonus interest rate. Easy.

  • Take money out at any time without affecting your bonus rate.
  • Open up to 10 Save accounts and get bonus interest on all of them,
    including shared Save accounts
  • Get bonus interest on combined savings of up to $250K across all your
    Save accounts. Your savings up to $100K will earn 5.50% p.a. and over
    $100K to $250K will earn 5.00% p.a.
  • Don’t miss out on bonus interest with the help of our friendly nudges.

Key changes to Save accounts from 1 July

Meet our new bonus rate criteria by depositing $500 monthly into any of your ubank accounts. Your combined
savings between $0-$100k will earn 5.50% p.a. and then your combined savings from $100K-$250K will earn
5.00% p.a. Just a reminder, most external payments from Save accounts will no longer be available.
